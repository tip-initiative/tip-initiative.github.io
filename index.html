About The TIP Initiative

The TIP initiative was created to develop and publish open transaction interface standards for the broadcast industry. Broadcasters, agencies, and system providers are encouraged to migrate to an updated common interface "language". Any player in the industry, or external to it, could choose to adopt the open standards, thereby pushing forward with innovation, automation, competition and interoperability.
